How can I add tasks?

Click on the "Add Task" button on the top right corner of the screen. Enter the task details and click on "Save".

How can I delete tasks?

Click on the "Delete" button on the task card. The task will be removed from the list.

How can I mark tasks as completed?

Click on the "Mark as Completed" button on the task card. The task will be marked as completed and moved to the "Completed" section.

How can I search for tasks?

Type the task title or description in the search bar on the top of the screen. The list will be filtered based on the search query.
